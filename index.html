Fake News Detection using Soft Computing (Neural Networks)

To develop a software system that classifies news as real or fake using natural language processing and machine learning.

Problem Statement

The rapid growth of digital media has led to the widespread dissemination of fake news, influencing public opinion and social stability. Manual verification is inefficient due to the high volume of data. Hence, an automated system is required to detect fake news accurately using computational techniques.

Literature Review

Studies show that linguistic patterns, contextual features, and deep learning models such as BERT and neural networks significantly improve fake news detection accuracy.

Research Gap

Our approach improves reliability by combining enhanced preprocessing with multiple machine learning models.

System Methodology

Dataset

Kaggle Fake News Dataset (20,000+ articles), cleaned using tokenization, stopword removal, and stemming.

Model

TF-IDF feature extraction with Neural Network and Logistic Regression classifiers.
